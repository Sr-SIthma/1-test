WhatsApp Web is the browser-based version of the popular messaging app WhatsApp. It allows users to send and receive messages, photos, videos, and voice notes directly from their computer by syncing with their mobile device. To use it, users simply scan a QR code from web.whatsapp.com using the WhatsApp app on their phone.
